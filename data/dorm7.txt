I lived in Hubbard for a year. I liked the convenience of having a Sparty’s, trash chutes on every floor, printing areas, bike rooms, and an ATM, but that’s about it. I didn’t like the location, rooms, or the immature freshman who get placed there.

I lived in Mayo for 3 years and loved it, for the most part. First of all, it’s an unbelievably gorgeous building. It’s the prettiest building on campus IMO. It’s also very newly renovated, so it feels fresh and beautiful, inside and out. It also has a great location, being across the street from downtown EL and not far from most key places on campus. I also like how much smaller and personal that Mayo is compared to something like Hubbard. It’s also usually very quiet and relaxed. Definitely a more mature atmosphere. The only thing I didn’t care for was the fact that there was no printing area downstairs, bike room, or ATM. I’d have to go to different buildings for those, but Mayo is still by far one of the best dorms on campus.
