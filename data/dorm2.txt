Case is LOUD. Lots of freshman first and foremost. Great location for sports/classes and no walk to a dining hall, but compared to other dorms it feels really cramped. On the 6th floor there is a really annoying ledge in the ceiling so you can’t put your beds against the wall. The window is also kind of small and the desk chairs are crumbling as we speak but overall I liked this dorm. The showers are a lot bigger than others you’ll find in suite style dorms and it looks like every MSU dorm ever.
The spartys is immaculate but the dining hall unfortunately serves the same food everyday. Gets old quickly besides GLP, and there’s no other dining halls in South Neighborhood. - 3 years ago
